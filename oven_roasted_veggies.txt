Preheat oven to 400F.
Chop some potatoes, carrots, and brussel sprouts and put them in a pyrex bowl.
Drizzle olive oil onto the chopped veggies. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Mix the oil, salt and pepper into the veggies.
Now add some rosemary leaves to the mix.
Put the bowl in the oven. Wait 30 mins and serve.
